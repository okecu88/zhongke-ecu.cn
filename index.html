公司简介

公司拥有专业化技术团队，先进的检测设备，完善的生产工艺，通过汽车零部件的检测研发，展览展示，产业基地，为国内外客商提供优质的附加服务。

并利用丰富的行业资源及影响力，开展产品，技术，市场和项目的咨询服务，公司凭借准确的市场定位和人性化管理，积累了丰富的生产管理和经营管理的经验，已发展成为集生产、研发、销售、国际贸易于一体的专业的汽车零部件生产企业。

The company has a professional technical team, advanced testing equipment, and a complete production process. Through the testing and R&D of auto parts, exhibition and display, and industrial base, we provide high-quality value-added services for domestic and foreign customers.

Leveraging abundant industry resources and influence, we provide consulting services covering products, technology, market and projects. With accurate market positioning and humanized management, the company has accumulated rich experience in production management and operation management, and has developed into a professional auto parts manufacturer integrating production, R&D, sales and international trade.
